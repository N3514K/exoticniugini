About Us

Exotic Niugini Tours is an inbound tour operator based in Port Moresby and is registered with the PNG Investment Promotion Authority as Exotic Niugini Tours #6-123277198. We specialise in soft adventure tours but also cater for special interest groups, nature-based team building programs for cooperate clients, shore excursions for cruise ships, and special excursion programs for church groups and schools.

Our Mission

To provide sustainable and immersive travel experiences while promoting environmental and cultural awareness in Papua New Guinea.

Our Goal

Providing unique life-enhancing exotic experiences while supporting environment, culture and wildlife protection.
