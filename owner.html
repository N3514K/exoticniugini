Meet the Owner

Wesley Momen, the owner of Exotic Niugini Tours, is a local Papua New Guinean born in the Trobriand Islands of Milne Bay Province. He developed an interest in tourism after working with various local and overseas tour operators as a tourist guide and tour coordinator for over 16 years.
